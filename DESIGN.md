# Journey to Mastery - Design System

## Design Philosophy

This website should feel like a modern Japanese experience rather than a gaming website.

Avoid:
- Heavy blood splatters
- Too many red elements
- Complex illustrations everywhere
- Multiple competing visuals
- Cyberpunk aesthetics

Aim for:
- Calm
- Elegant
- Premium
- Minimal
- Japanese editorial design
- Luxury portfolio website feel

Reference:
Use the attached design as the primary inspiration.

# Design Restrictions

DO NOT USE:

- Neon colors
- Heavy gradients
- Glassmorphism
- Blood effects
- Anime style visuals
- Gaming UI

USE:

- Editorial layouts
- Japanese minimalism
- Soft shadows
- Elegant whitespace
- Clean typography

The final result should look closer to a luxury architecture studio website than a hackathon website.
